Thumbas — Digitalizing Traditional Markets

THE PROBLEM

Traditional market traders are often marginalized by modern e-commerce and digital marts. This creates an economic gap for local sellers who lack digital access.

THE SOLUTION

Thumbas is a UI/UX prototype designed to bridge traditional traders with digital consumers. It focuses on maintaining the affordable essence of traditional markets while providing the convenience of modern logistics.
